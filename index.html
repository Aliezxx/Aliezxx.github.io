True Stories

Jason, a primary schoool student, wants to earn some pocket money to buy himself a set of comic books. He finds a task which needs him to walk dog every afternoon and water the plants three times a week. He earns 50 dollars after working for three weeks and buy the comic books successfully. His parents agree to let him earn pocket money because they trust their neighbors. And the app slakes their demand.
Colin, a programmer in a technical company, is always busy working wherever at home or in the company. He likes to shop online to buy everything he needs in daily life since he has no time to go to the shopping mall. So there are always many parcels waiting for him to bring home. Therefore he finds his neighbor on the app and let him to take place picking up the parcels. That way saves him a lot of time with little cost.
